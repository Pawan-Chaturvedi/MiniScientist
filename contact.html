Booking FAQs

Common questions about booking

How soon should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for better availability. Last-minute bookings (within 1-2 weeks) may be available based on schedule.

What's your response time?

We respond to all booking inquiries within 24 hours. For WhatsApp messages, we usually reply within 2 hours during business hours.

Can I book on a specific date?

Yes! We work around your preferred date. Our team coordinates with you to confirm availability and finalize the event schedule.

What if I need to reschedule?

Rescheduling is possible up to 15 days before your event with no additional charge. After that, a rescheduling fee may apply.

Do you service areas outside Indore?

Yes! We service the greater Indore region. Travel charges may apply for locations outside city limits. Contact us for details.

What happens after I submit the form?

We'll review your request and call/WhatsApp you within 24 hours with a personalized quote, availability, and next steps.
