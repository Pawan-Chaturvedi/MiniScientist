Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our science activities

What age groups can participate?

Our activities are designed for children aged 3-14. Each activity has specific age recommendations to ensure maximum engagement and safety.

Are the experiments safe?

Yes! All our experiments use non-toxic, child-safe materials and are conducted by trained professionals. We follow strict safety protocols.

Can parents participate?

Absolutely! We encourage parental involvement and supervision. It's a great way to bond and discuss science with your child.

Do children get to take projects home?

Yes, most hands-on activities include take-home projects like slime, crystals, or their creations from the experiment.

How long are the activities?

Duration varies from 30 minutes (shows) to 120 minutes (workshops). Check individual activity details or contact us for specifics.

Can activities be customized?

Yes! We can customize activities based on your event needs, theme, age group, and preferences. Contact us for custom options.
