Our Story

From passion for science to inspiring young scientists

Mini Scientist was founded with a simple belief: Science should be fun, accessible, and magical for every child.

We started as a small team of passionate educators and scientists who noticed that traditional science education often fails to spark curiosity in children. We wanted to change that. We wanted to create experiences where science comes alive through interactive demonstrations, hands-on experiments, and themed adventures.

Today, we're proud to have brought science to life for thousands of children at birthday parties, school assemblies, and special events across the region. Each child who participates in our activities becomes a young scientist, learning real concepts while having incredible fun.

Our commitment remains unchanged: to inspire, educate, and create memories that last a lifetime.
